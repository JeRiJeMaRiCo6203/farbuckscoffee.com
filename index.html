FArbucks

An Indonesian multinational coffee house and roaster chain headquartered in Jakarta, Indonesia. One of the largest coffee shop chain in the world.

As of June 2022, the company has 12,556 stores in 50 countries. “FArbucks” derived from the words "far" and "bucks", which implies that the company will go as far as a herd of bucks.
