About Us

HISTORY

It all starts in 2021, when Jerico, our founder, was struck by the sad news of his grandfather's passing. Jerico, who has been with his grandfather taking care of the coffee plantation since childhood, then entrusted with managing the coffee plantation that his grandfather left behind. The coffee plantation, which is located 1,500-1,700 meters above sea level, is located on the hillside of Rante Karua, North Toraja, South Sulawesi.

By prioritizing the involvement of the surrounding community, this garden is well managed from budding, maintenance, harvesting to post-harvest. High attention to quality is not only focused on coffee plantations in Toraja, the production process until the product can be consumed by consumers is also well calculated. The product technology uses the most advanced technology to ensure the best taste of coffee can be produced and packaged so that quality is maintained.

INNOVATION

While continuing his grandfather's coffee plantation business, Jerico studied at Binus University. There, he met a friend and business partner to this day. That person is Geren Widiarta. Both of them, who have a background in computer science, then had a brilliant idea to create a website for the advancement of their coffee plantation business.

The name "FArbucks" which is taken from the words "far" and "bucks" was chosen to answer the ideals of its founders, which is to be able to go far like a herd of bucks. And in december 2021, the FArbucks Coffee website was created. FArbucks, which is actually a producer of high quality coffee, then expanded its business by selling a wide selection of amazing coffee drinks.
